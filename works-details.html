Clipp

Clipp is an interactive peer-to-peer marketplace designed for college students to trade second-hand items at no monetary cost. Clipp encompasses a gamified swiping model along with a serendipity effect to create a new and unique marketplace for college students. With no searching algorithm, Clipp gamifies traditional browsing methods with a new bartering and exchange element.

The app also diverges from conventional online marketplaces in which users will never encounter shipping fees, seller fees, bargaining, and other typical frictions. As a completely cashless experience, Clipp seeks to eliminate waste while also being the go-to app for college students who wish to receive a secondhand item in exchange for an item he/she no longer has a need for.

The app has 5 core features:
1. Swiping Model
2. Personalized Profile
3. User Rating System
4. Direct Messaging
5. Scheduling Features
