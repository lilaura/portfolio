Hi, I am Laura.

I’m an undergraduate student currently studying User Experience Design at the University of Michigan, where I develop a systematic UX research and design tool set.

My UX internships were mostly in small teams, where I've learned to be proactive, communicate with different roles in the team, and take more responsibility of the product from a user experience perspective.

I have a background in programming, where I learned C++, data structures and algorithms. In th learning process, I coded a C++ project that simulates optimal stock exchange in real-time across different commodities, and monitors and analyzes trader data. I also coded a C++ project that manages massive size log files, and quickly search, sort, archive information from log files.
I also have some experience with Python, where I've worked on projects with APIs, data manipulation and visualization, databases and SQLite,natural language processing, etc.
